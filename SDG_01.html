Global understanding of poverty

During the last 25 years, extreme global poverty has declined steadily from 34 percent in 1990 to 10 percent in 2015, which represents the scape of 1.2 billion people out of extreme poverty. Yet, this remarkable success has not been achieved equally around the world, neither does it mean that those who have escaped enjoy a significantly better quality of life.
